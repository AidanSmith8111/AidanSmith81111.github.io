Travel to Belize!

Where there is lots of good history, Culture, sights, activities, and food!

Belize is a country located on the coast of Central America with shorelines to the east and dense jungles to the west. Offshore Belize has a dozen of islands called cayes. It has amazing sea life and is home of the Mayan ruins!

History

Before it became the Colony of British Honduras, Belize was home of the ancient Mayan civilizations. In the 16th and 17th centuries, Spain and Britain both attempted to colonize their land. The British ended up settling in their land in the 1710s. They kept expanding their trade and eventually gain complete rule over Belize. This is why their culture seems to be over the place. Culture

The culture of Belize is highly diverse. It is a mixture of Mayan, Kriol, East Indian, and alot more! Here are some of their traditions:

  1. They celebrate Christmas during the entire month of December.

  3. They're the orgins of Punta(a type of music).
