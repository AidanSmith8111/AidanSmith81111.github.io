Travel to Belize!

Where there is lots of good Culture, sights, activities, and food!

Belize is a country located on the coast of Central America with shorelines to the east and dense jungles to the west. Offshore Belize has a dozen of islands called cayes. It has amazing sea life and is home of the Mayan ruins!

Culture

The culture of Belize is highly diverse. It is a mixture of Mayan, Kriol, East Indian, and alot more! Here are some of their traditions:

  1. They celebrate Christmas during the entire month of December.
  2. Their Independence day is September 21.
  3. They're the orgins of Punta(a type of music).
